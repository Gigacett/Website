The Climate Change

The result of climate change can be seen thought out the world. One of the most supported theory is that green house gases is trapping the heat that would normally radiate to outer space. As the green house gases build up in our atomsphere they start to form this dense layer of particles and reflecting heat back to the surface. Some of the green house gases are CO2, Methane, Nitrous Oxide and CFCs. And based on the data provided by NASA there has been a clear increase in the CO2 parts per million during the last hundred years that human has been on earth. This is a clear indiction that there does exist some man made contribution to the issue of climate change.Althought the situation might seem dire, it is possible to fix the issue if the general population starts to act now. One of the biggest contributor to the climate change is the burning of fossil fuel, by going green or nuclear we can avoid putting more green house gas into our atomsphere. An other simple solution is to start looking toward space exploration.
