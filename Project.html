The Planet Earth

The earth is home to the human species and the only planet that can support human life at the time. However during the recent years there has been an rise in temperature and this is casuing irreversible change to our one and only home. The earth is currently 4.5 billion years old while humans have only existed for 6 million years. But in the last few hundred of years, the Earth is reaching a new high in its average temperature around the global. This have the potential effect of more frequent wildfires, longer periods of drought, and more intense storm. While the Glaciers is sinking and risk a flooding of the coastal cities around the world. This can have dire consequences on the human speices as a whole.
